Pagefetch

Pagefetch me if you can is a game developed as a part of a research project in the school of computing science in the University of Glasgow.
The project aims to determine how easily different pages are to find, how well people can query, and whether it is possible to estimate how easy or how hard is it to find these pages using measures of findability.
