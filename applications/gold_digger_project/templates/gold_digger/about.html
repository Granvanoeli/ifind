About Gold Digger




Gold Digger is a project by the University of Glasgow developed by Gabriele Rossi and supervised by Leif Azzopardi. It's objective is to provide a game that would act as a metaphor for searching behavior. In the same way as users of a web search engine tipe in queries to the system, so do the miners in gold digger access a mine showing different layers instead of different links. Each layer/link will hold a certain amount of gold just as each link in a web search will hold a certain amount of information that might be more or less useful to us. With this game/experiment we hope to find a correspondence between the two behaviors and hopefully let users have fun!
